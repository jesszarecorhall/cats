Cats!

My family has lots of cats! The cat in the top left is my parent's cat named Cinder. The top right through middle right are my sisters three cats named Charlie, Norman, and Suzy. The last cat on the bottom row is my cat named Luna. She is not super photogenic which is why we have a lot of funny pictures of her.

Why you should get a cat

  1. They are so cute!
  2. They love their humans
  3. They do fun stuff all the time like...
    • Cuddle you
    • Say hi when you get home
    • Follow you around to see what you are doing

Funny Stories About the Cats in My Family

  • Cinder: My parent's cat, Cinder, loves to eat. Because of this she has become quite hefty so my little brother has given her the title "Cinder-block".
  • Norman, Charlie, and Suzy: My sister's three cats are pure trouble, but since they are so cute they always get out of trouble. Suzy was the last cat to be added to their family and she has taught her brothers some crazy activities. One of these activities is she has taught them how to open their treat container (which is a sealed tupperware container) and bite through the treat packages to get their midnight snack.
  • Luna: My cat has become a professional masseuse. I woke up sleeping on my stomach one night and my neck was hurting a lot from the weird position. Luna came over and started kneading my neck and it actually made it feel better!
